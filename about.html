About Leviathan Drones



We are a team of five passionate engineers and innovators committed to developing an affordable underwater drone for a wide range of applications, including marine exploration, environmental research, and underwater inspections. Driven by our goal to make underwater technology accessible to everyone, we are not only building a high-quality, cost-effective drone, but also offering an affordable course that teaches you how to build it yourself. Whether you prefer a hands-on approach or a ready-to-use product, you can join us in exploring the depths with cutting-edge technology designed for both professionals and enthusiasts alike.

Our mission is to inspire curiosity, support learning, and help you uncover the mysteries beneath the surface with confidence and ease.
