What Will You Build

Leviathan Drone is a fully functional ,3d printed underwater drone...



85 Meters Depth

We’ve tested Drone 5 up to simulated pressure of 85 meters underwater. It’s possible with our own watertight endcap design.

3D Printed

The majority of the drone is 3D printed – the entirety of the shell, propellers, mounts as well as water-proof endcaps.





Fast & Maneuverable

With top speed of 2m/s, CPS 5 is really maneuverable. It also has active self-leveling

Live Video Feed

QGroundControl app displays all of the data and the 1080p live video feed from the drone’s camera onboard
